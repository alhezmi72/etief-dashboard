Emerging Technology Exploration & Assessment Framework (ETIEF)

Purpose and Objectives

The framework aims to

  • Systematically identify short-term (1-year horizon) and long-term (5-year horizon) emerging technologies.
  • Evaluate their potential business, operational and societal impact.
  • Prioritize technologies for R&D, partnerships, and innovation investments.
  • Guide strategic decision-making for competitive differentiation and efficiency.

Framework Structure

The ETIEF can be divided into four major stages

  • Scanning & Identification – Discover potential emerging technologies.
  • Assessment & Classification – Evaluate and categorize them by maturity and impact.
  • Prioritization & Roadmapping – Align technologies with strategic goals and time horizons.
  • Action & Monitoring – Recommend actions, track progress, and update insights.
