Consolidation Algorithm

Assessment Methodology

Tech Impact Score (TIS) Formula

TIS = 0.35×Impact + 0.30×Strategic Fit + 0.20×(10-Barriers) + 0.15×(10-Sustainability)

Five Evaluation Dimensions

  • Technology Maturity (TRL): Readiness level 1-9
  • Business Impact: Revenue growth, cost reduction potential
  • Strategic Fit: Alignment with QMIC vision & mission
  • Adoption Barriers: Regulatory, skills, infrastructure challenges
  • Sustainability: ESG impact, circular economy potential
