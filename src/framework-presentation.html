Solution

Framework Approach

4-Stage systematic methodology for technology intelligence

🔍
Stage 1 — Scanning
Multi-source technology identification via Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and DeepSeek working in parallel consensus.
📊
Stage 2 — Assessment
Weighted scoring across 5 dimensions: TRL, Impact, Strategic Fit, Barriers, and Sustainability.
🎯
Stage 3 — Prioritization
Portfolio matrix classification and roadmapping across short, mid, and long-term time horizons.
⚡
Stage 4 — Action
Implementation planning with KPIs, governance structures, and continuous monitoring loops.

Assessment Framework

Five-dimensional scoring for rigorous technology evaluation

TIS = 0.35 × Impact  +  0.30 × Strategic Fit  +  0.20 × (10 − Barriers)  +  0.15 × (10 − Sustainability Cost)
